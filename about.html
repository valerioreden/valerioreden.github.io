Background

I am an independent developer based in Philippines. I graduated senior high at STI College Malolos. There, I was introduced to programming and discovered a new world of amazing and fun technologies I can mess and learn from. Right then, I started diving the rabbit hole of programming. I learned many things through creating and building solutions to the problems I encounter. Everyday I always learn something new. As I continue my journey, I realized that I gained enough experience to offer my services to other people. I was able to become a developer through the passion I have for learning and the dedication I have to accomplish my goals.

As an independent developer, I work with other people (mostly students) by offerring my services through freelancing. I mostly work with desktop applications (Software Development) and gameplay and graphical aspects of games (Game Development).
I also develop tools to improve my workflows and help boost my productivity as a developer. Most of my past freelance projects are small desktop applications (CRUD) made with Java.

If I am not working on a freelance project, I am creating small game prototypes or hacking small projects to further hone my skills. Currently, I have one shipped game (Thesis) I worked on with my client and a bunch of throwaway projects hidden from the outside world. Most of my games are prototypes or game jam entries which are really fun to make and learn from.
